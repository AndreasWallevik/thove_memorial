Remembering Her

Thove was a gentle and steady presence, someone who made every room feel calmer and every person feel welcome. She carried grace in the everyday moments and gave her care freely.

She had a way of listening that made people feel truly seen. Her laughter, patience, and devotion to family created a home full of warmth that continues to guide us.

We miss her deeply, and we are forever grateful for her love. Through memories shared here, we honor her life and keep her spirit close.
